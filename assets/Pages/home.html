Welcome to NB Care

New Beginnings Bristol Ltd is a friendly support agency offering support to Mental health issues. Our aim is to help people be as independent as possible; offering support, practical advice on social events to build confidence and achieve goals. We provide dedicated one to one support promoting independence whilst supporting in their own homes with all aspects of daily living, building their confidence to get involved with their local community around them. We offer a range of support services, including a monthly Disco that gives people the chance to meet their friends and enjoy the social atmosphere with a drink in a welcoming environment. Please give us a call if you’d like more information.

Our People

Our people – caring for the people that matter most in the world to you – really set NEW BEGINNINGS apart. As well as being highly skilled and professional, they are compassionate and understanding, helping to create the ‘family’ environments that are so important for residents to feel safe and settled. NEW BEGINNINGS’s teams are warm, empathetic and committed to delivering the very best possible care, recognising the individual in everyone. Kindness and support extend to residents’ families and friends too, with a warm welcome at every visit and regular updates on how your loved one is doing.
